Opporteenities


Opporteenities is a website that features opportunities for teens. Opporteenities aims to show teens that they can make an impact on their community or start working towards their dream career now. Everyone should take advantage of every opportunity that comes their way. On Opporteenities you can find volunteer opportunities, internships, programs, and resources to learn something new.



Volunteer


Volunteering is a great way to give back to your community and help others. You are able to connect with your community by doing something good. You have the opportunity to make a positive impact on someone's life or your community. There are many different ways of volunteering that can be really fun! Volunteering allows you to explore your passions while doing something good. Volunteering can teach you valuable life lessons and give you some great experience. Lots of organizations could use your help, so start volunteering today!



Internships and Programs


Internships and programs are great ways to learn about a specific career or to gain experience for a future job. Internships and programs can be year-long, semester-long, or only during the summer. Internships are usually when a student works in a professional environment to learn and gain experience. Internships can be paid or unpaid. Programs can be at universities, museums, online and more. Programs are where students learn new skills and can explore new career paths. Programs can be free or cost money. Internships and programs are great ways to prepare for a future job, explore passions, and they look great on resumes. Start learning and working towards your future career today!



Learn


Start learning something new today! Whether you want to learn a new programming language, self study for an AP test, take an online college-level course, or need help with school work, there are lots of available recourses. There are many websites that offer free or low cost classes that you can take from the comfort of your home. You can learn almost anything online from a new language to the basics of data science. Take challenging classes and recieve a certificate, or take a fun class that you have always dreamed of taking. Start learning today!
