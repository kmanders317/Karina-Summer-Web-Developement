Learn!


Start learning something new today! Whether you want to learn a new programming language, self study for an AP test, take an online college-level course, or need help with school work, there are lots of available recourses.







Khan Academy


Khan Academy is a great online learning recourse. It has a large selection of classes in math, science, computing, arts and humanities, and reading and language arts. It also has great SAT practice quizes. Students learn and practice at their own pace and get help from recorded videos. Khan Academy is completely free.

Learn More



EdX

EdX is a great website that offers free college courses from universities and companies. There is a huge selection of classes on EdX. Some classes are taught live, while others are pre-recorded and self paced. Some courses offer an official certificate for completing the class for some money. This is a great place to take challenging classes for free.

Learn More



Coursera

Coursera is a website with a great selection of online courses. Many courses are from universities. You are able to audit classes for free, but to get a certificate and to have access to all graded assignments, you must have a membership. Coursera certificates are a great addition to your resume. Learning something new is easy with Coursera.
